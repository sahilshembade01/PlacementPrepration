Discussion Tips for Placement Group Discussions

  • Be a Good Listener - Focus on others' points before responding.
  • Stay Relevant and to the Point - Avoid straying from the topic.
  • Speak Confidently - Express your thoughts clearly.
  • Avoid Interrupting - Wait your turn and listen attentively.
  • Maintain Body Language - Eye contact and good posture matter.
  • Take Initiative - Contribute and steer the conversation when necessary.
  • Respect Everyone’s Opinion - Disagree politely with reasons.

Important Questions for Group Discussions

  • What qualities make a good leader?
  • How important is team management for organizational success?
  • What are the key challenges of gender equality in the workplace?
  • How should society address the issue of rising mental health concerns?
  • Will automation replace jobs or create new opportunities?
  • How can businesses ensure they stay competitive in the digital era?
  • How do personal values align with professional growth?
